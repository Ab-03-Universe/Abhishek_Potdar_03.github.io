About

I am a physics researcher working in radio astronomy and solar physics. My research focuses on solar radio bursts, flare energetics, and the development of radio astronomy instrumentation.

I developed a 3-meter automated radio telescope operating at 1420 MHz for neutral hydrogen observations.

Research Interests

  • Solar radio bursts (Type II and Type III)
  • Solar flare timing and energetics
  • Radio telescope instrumentation
  • Hydrogen 21-cm observations

Projects

3-Meter Radio Telescope

Design and development of an automated 3-meter parabolic radio telescope operating at 1.42 GHz using SDR spectrometer.

Solar Radio Burst Analysis

Statistical analysis of temporal association between solar flares and radio bursts.

Feedhorn Design

Simulation and development of VE4MA feedhorn and choke-ring structures for radio astronomy receivers.
