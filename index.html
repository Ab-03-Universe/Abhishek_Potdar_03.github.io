About

I am a physics researcher working in radio astronomy and solar physics. My research focuses on the temporal association between solar flares and radio bursts, and on the development of radio astronomy instrumentation.

I developed a 3-meter automated radio telescope operating at 1420 MHz for neutral hydrogen observations and radio astronomy experiments.

Research Interests

  • Solar radio bursts (Type II and Type III)
  • Solar flare energetics and timing
  • Radio instrumentation and receiver design
  • Neutral hydrogen 21-cm observations

Projects

3-Meter Radio Telescope

Design and development of an automated 3-meter radio telescope operating at 1.42 GHz using SDR spectrometer.

Solar Radio Burst Analysis

Statistical analysis of Type II and Type III radio bursts and their temporal association with solar flares.

Feedhorn Design

Design and simulation of VE4MA feedhorn and choke ring structures for radio astronomy receivers.
