About

I am a physics researcher working in radio astronomy and solar physics. My research focuses on solar radio bursts, flare energetics, and radio instrumentation.

I developed a 3-meter automated radio telescope operating at 1420 MHz for hydrogen line observations.

Research Interests

  • Solar radio bursts (Type II and Type III)
  • Solar flare timing and radio emission
  • Radio telescope instrumentation
  • Hydrogen 21-cm observations

Projects

3-Meter Radio Telescope

Design and development of a 3-meter automated radio telescope operating at 1.42 GHz using SDR-based spectrometer.

Solar Radio Burst Analysis

Statistical study of the temporal association between solar flares and radio bursts including Type II and Type III bursts.
